# Enterprise AI Visibility

Enterprise AI adoption is accelerating faster than most companies can track, audit, or secure it.

Across teams, employees are using AI tools, building workflows, and creating internal applications—often without visibility, governance, or oversight.

This creates a new problem:

**AI usage exists. Visibility does not.**

---

## The Core Problem

Most enterprises cannot answer:

- How do we track AI usage across employees and departments?
- Where is shadow AI happening?
- Have we audited actual AI usage?
- What AI tools exist across the company?
- What does real usage data show?

Without these answers, governance is guesswork.

---
