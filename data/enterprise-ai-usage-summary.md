# Enterprise AI Usage Summary

This summary is based on analysis of enterprise AI usage across 429 respondents.

---

## Key Observations

- AI adoption is widespread across teams  
- Multiple AI tools per employee is common  
- Usage is driven bottom-up, not centrally managed  
- Tracking of AI usage is minimal or non-existent  
- Governance policies lag behind actual behavior  
- Sensitive data is being shared with AI tools  

---

## Core Insight

AI usage is accelerating faster than enterprise visibility.

Organizations do not have a clear understanding of:

- Who is using AI  
- What tools are being used  
- What data is being shared  
- Where risks exist  

---

## The Gap

There is a growing gap between:

**AI Usage → and → AI Visibility**

This gap creates:

- Security risks  
- Compliance challenges  
- Lack of control  

---

## What This Means

Organizations must:

- Track AI usage  
- Detect shadow AI  
- Audit usage  
- Discover tools  

Before governance can be effective.
