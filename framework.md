# Enterprise AI Visibility Framework

## Overview

Enterprise AI adoption is decentralized, fast, and often invisible.

Employees across teams are using AI tools, building workflows, and creating internal applications without going through traditional development or approval processes.

This breaks the assumptions that most governance and security systems rely on.

Before enforcing policies, organizations need visibility.

---

## The Sequence

Effective enterprise AI management follows this sequence:

**Track → Detect → Audit → Discover → Govern**

Most companies attempt to start at governance.

This leads to incomplete control and hidden risk.

---

## 1. Track AI Usage

### Goal  
Understand how AI is being used across employees and departments.

### Includes  
- Tools being used (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, internal tools)  
- Frequency and patterns of usage  
- Data inputs and outputs  

### Why it matters  
Tracking establishes a baseline. Without it, all decisions are assumptions.

---

## 2. Detect Shadow AI

### Goal  
Identify AI usage that exists outside approved or visible systems.

### Includes  
- External AI tools used without approval  
- Internal workflows built using AI  
- API-based integrations and automations  

### Why it matters  
Shadow AI introduces risk through invisible data flows and unmonitored usage.

---

## 3. Audit AI Usage

### Goal  
Create a complete and accurate picture of AI usage.

### Includes  
- Teams using AI  
- Tools and use cases  
- Data exposure and risk areas  

### Why it matters  
An audit replaces assumptions with ground truth.

---

## 4. Discover AI Tools

### Goal  
Identify all AI tools used across the organization.

### Challenge  
AI adoption is bottom-up and decentralized. There is no central registry.

### Why it matters  
You cannot manage or secure tools you do not know exist.

---

## 5. Govern

### Goal  
Apply policies, controls, and monitoring to AI usage.

### Includes  
- Access controls  
- Data handling policies  
- Monitoring and compliance  

### Important  
Governance without visibility is ineffective.

---

## Key Insight

Enterprise AI does not have an adoption problem.

It has a visibility problem.

Organizations that prioritize visibility first will be able to govern, secure, and optimize AI effectively.
